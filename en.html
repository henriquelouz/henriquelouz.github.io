About me

My name is Henrique Almeida Louzada, born and living in Belo Horizonte - MG, Brazil. I've been a programmer since 2015, the same year I graduated as a computer technician from COTEMIG. In 2021, I finished the bachelor's degree in Information Systems at PUC Minas, for which I received the Brazilian Computing Society Outstanding Student Award, participated in research groups and published award-winning articles in the areas of HCI and computer vision. I am currently taking a Master's degree in Computer Science at UFMG, contributing to the CIIA-Saúde data parity project in partnership with Unimed and the City Hall of Belo Horizonte. I work as a Machine Learning Engineer at Itaú Unibanco, where I am guiding my career towards the management area.
